Emotion Recognition

Emotion Recognition from speech using Deep Learning.

Speech is the result of convolved signal produced by the vocal cord and the vocal tract. Recognizing the feelings others are trying to convey through speech is essential. There are a various parameters of speech signal that defines the feelings. Thus, speech emotion recognition (SER) from the speech signal is a challenging task. In this paper proposed a SER system based on the features extracted and obtained by Mel-Frequency Cepstral Coefficient (MFCC), spectrograms. The 1-dimensional CNN model architecture is implemented in this paper. The work is performed on the "The Ryerson Audio-Visual Database of Emotional Speech and Song (RAVDESS)" dataset.6 emotions based on their gender are classified (i.e. 6 x 2 emotions) with 82.3% of accuracy. The emotions classified are happy, sad, angry, calm, fear and nervous.
